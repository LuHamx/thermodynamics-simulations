What's happening?

Think of the gas inside as a bunch of tiny, bouncy super-balls. When you add heat,
those balls start zooming around way faster and slamming into the piston!
Because the piston can move, the gas actually pushes it up. That "push" is the gas doing Thermal Work.
It's like the heat is giving the gas muscles to lift a heavy weight!
