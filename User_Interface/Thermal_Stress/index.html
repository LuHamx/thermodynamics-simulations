Formula: σ = EαΔT
ΔT: °C
Stress: MPa

What's happening?

Imagine you're trying to stretch out your arms, but you're trapped in a tiny metal box.
You want to grow, but the walls say "NO!" That "pushing" feeling is Thermal Stress.
When an object gets hot and wants to expand, but something (like a frame or bolts) holds it back,
the object builds up huge pressure inside. If it gets too hot, it might even bend or snap!
