What's happening?

Imagine a perfectly clean room where every toy is neatly lined up.
Now imagine a bunch of hyper kids running around throwing everything everywhere!
That's kind of what Entropy is, the particles become more random and disorganized as temperature increases.
When heat is added, the particles gain energy, move more wildly, and spread out in many different directions.
Higher entropy means the system becomes more chaotic and less ordered over time!
